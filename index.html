About

Biospectra is a national level inter college technical symposium organized by the Biomedical Engineers’ Association. Starting form the very first edition of Biospectra in April 2012, the flagship event proves to be grand success year by year. The symposium comprises of 15+ technical and non-technical events, various domain workshops, guest lectures, carnival, project expo and medexpo. The symposium has an average foot fall of 800+ participants from all over the country.

1. Paper Presentation

Participants submit a paper on the research perspective or completed project in IEEE format. After shortlisting, a presentation is conferred to the jury.

2. Poster Presentation

A poster based on the given domains is presented, as a part of a future project.

3. Spark out

An elecfest which tests the technical skills by letting the smart brains go rampant on the stage of electrical circuits and electronic devices.

4. Diagnose it

An quizzing event to test the knowledge of participants in medical diagnosis, disease, symptoms and the technology available for its cure.

5. Bionomics

An event to kindle the entrepreneur skills in healthcare field.

6. Project expo

Participants display their projects and explain the technology they have adopted as well as how they built it.

7. Workshops

Some of the interesting workshops which was conducted listed in the following : 3D printing , ECG kit development , design thinking, introduction to python and ethical hacking.
